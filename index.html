Arman Afrasiyabi

Université Laval & Mila
I am a Ph.D. candidate at Université Laval and Mila group supervised by Prof. Jean-François Lalonde and Prof. Christian Gagné. I am working on meta-learning, and unsupervised representation (self-supervised) learning in low supervision conditions. My current research is focused on contextual self-supervised learning for quick adaptations.

I was born and raised in Band, a beautiful village near Urmia City.
