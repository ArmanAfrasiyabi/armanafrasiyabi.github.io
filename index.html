Arman Afrasiyabi

Mila -Québec AI Institute · Université Laval

Hi, I am a final year Ph.D. candidate at Mila and Université Laval supervised by Prof. Jean-François Lalonde and Prof. Christian Gagné. I am working on meta-learning, representation learning, and self-supervised learning in low supervision conditions. My current research is focused on contextual self-supervised learning for quick adaptations.

I was born and raised in Band, a beautiful village near Urmia City.
